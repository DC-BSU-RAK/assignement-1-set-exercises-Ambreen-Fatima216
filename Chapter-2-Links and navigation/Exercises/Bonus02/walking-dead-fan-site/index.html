A show about zombies? No. A show about survival, humanity, and how far people will go when the world turns to hell.

Created by: Frank Darabont

Based on: The Walking Dead comics by Robert Kirkman, Tony Moore, and Charlie Adlard

Original Network: AMC

Show Premiere: October 31, 2010

Show Finale: November 20, 2022

Seasons: 11

Episodes: 177

"At first glance, The Walking Dead might seem like a show about surviving the undead — the terrifying walkers that roam the world. But look closer, and you'll see it's really about the people. The real danger isn’t always the zombies... it’s each other."

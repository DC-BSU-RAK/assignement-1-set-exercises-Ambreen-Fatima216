A show about zombies? Hardly. The Walking Dead is a story about survival, identity, and the moral lines people cross when the world collapses around them.

Created by: Frank Darabont

Based on: The Walking Dead comics by Robert Kirkman, Tony Moore, and Charlie Adlard

Original Network: AMC

Premiere Date: October 31, 2010

Final Episode: November 20, 2022

Total Seasons: 11

Total Episodes: 177

At first glance, it’s a series about surviving flesh-eating walkers — but beneath the blood and chaos, The Walking Dead is a raw exploration of human nature. Who do we become when everything is stripped away? The dead may walk, but it’s the living you should fear.

“My mercy prevails over my wrath.” — Rick Grimes

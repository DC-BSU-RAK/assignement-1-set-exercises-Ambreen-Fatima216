Main Cast Season 1-11

  • Rick Grimes Played By Andrew Lincoln - A sheriff turned leader, fighting for his group's survival.
  • Daryl Dixon Played By Norman Reedus - A quiet, loyal survivor with a heart of gold.
  • Carl Grimes Played By Chandler Riggs - A young boy growing into a strong, courageous survivor.
  • Negan Smith Played By Jeffrey Dean Morgan - Charismatic villain turned complex anti-hero seeking redemption.
  • Michonne Played By Danai Gurira - A fierce, independent warrior evolving into a trusted leader.
  • Maggie Greene Played By Lauren Cohan - A farmer's daughter turned strong, resilient leader.
  • Glenn Rhee Played By Steven Yeun - Resourceful and loyal, sacrificing everything for his loved ones.
  • Carol Peletier Played By Melissa McBride - A strategic, fierce protector who evolves throughout the series.
  • Rosita Espinosa Played By Christian Serratos - A skilled fighter, loyal to her group and mission.
  • Ezekiel Played By Khary Payton - A charismatic leader with unwavering faith and strength.

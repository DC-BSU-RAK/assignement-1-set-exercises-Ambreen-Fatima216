Main Cast Season 1-11

  • Rick Grimes (Andrew Lincoln) – A former sheriff's deputy who wakes up in a hospital to find the world overtaken by the undead. Rick evolves from a hopeful protector to a hardened leader willing to make brutal choices to keep his people alive. His leadership is marked by tragedy, sacrifice, and the constant struggle to hold onto his humanity.
  • Daryl Dixon (Norman Reedus) – Once a lone wolf with a troubled past, Daryl becomes the moral compass of the group. Skilled in tracking and combat, he’s loyal to a fault, particularly to Rick and Carol. Despite his rough exterior, Daryl shows deep emotional growth and becomes a symbol of resilience and quiet strength.
  • Carl Grimes (Chandler Riggs) – Rick’s son, who grows up in a brutal, post-apocalyptic world. His journey from innocent child to wise young man is filled with hardship and difficult choices. Carl's vision for a better future, even after seeing so much death, leaves a lasting impact on the survivors — especially his father.
  • Negan Smith (Jeffrey Dean Morgan) – Introduced as the brutal leader of the Saviors, Negan is both feared and admired for his charisma and twisted sense of justice. He blurs the lines between villain and anti-hero, and his redemption arc — marked by deep reflection and genuine regret — becomes one of the most compelling stories in the show.
  • Michonne (Danai Gurira) – A katana-wielding warrior with a traumatic past. Initially a silent, stoic figure, Michonne gradually opens up and becomes one of Rick’s most trusted allies and eventually, his partner. Her strength, intelligence, and sense of justice make her one of the show’s most iconic characters.
  • Maggie Greene (Lauren Cohan) – Introduced as a farmer’s daughter at Hershel’s farm, Maggie grows into a fierce and principled leader. She suffers devastating losses, including Glenn, her husband, yet never loses her will to fight for a better world. Her courage and determination are unwavering throughout the series.
  • Glenn Rhee (Steven Yeun) – Once a resourceful pizza delivery guy, Glenn becomes a pillar of strength and compassion in the group. Known for his quick thinking and selflessness, Glenn often puts others before himself. His relationship with Maggie provides one of the show’s most heartfelt and tragic love stories.
  • Carol Peletier (Melissa McBride) – Beginning as a quiet, abused housewife, Carol undergoes one of the most dramatic transformations in the series. She becomes a cunning and fearless survivor who’s willing to make impossible decisions. Her journey is one of pain, loss, and ultimately, power.
  • Rosita Espinosa (Christian Serratos) – A skilled and pragmatic fighter who survives multiple losses and challenges. Rosita proves her worth time and time again with her strength, intelligence, and unwavering dedication to protecting her loved ones, even when it costs her dearly.
  • King Ezekiel (Khary Payton) – A former zookeeper who creates a community called The Kingdom and rules with theatrical flair, aided by his pet tiger, Shiva. Beneath his royal persona is a man full of hope, pain, and integrity. Ezekiel's optimism and leadership style set him apart in a world dominated by fear.
